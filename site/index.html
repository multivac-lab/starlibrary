Welcome to the Starlibrary

This is a deliberately small experiment.

How to ask good questions (that produce good answers)

A good question reduces ambiguity. It’s not about being verbose — it’s about being *well-posed*.

Control signals in frozen LLMs (a pragmatic view)

A “control signal” is any *input-side change* that reliably shifts a model’s behavior without changing the model’s weights.

Observable universe (no mysticism)

The **observable universe** is not “everything that exists”. It’s everything that can have had time to affect us *since the Big Bang*, given the finite speed of light and the expansion history of space.
